Work Experience

Between 2010 and 2013 I worked as an English teacher at La Araucana Educational Corporation. I was the homeroom teacher, responsible for groups of 45 students. I maintained contact with each of them, addressing their problems, needs, and interacting with their parents/guardians in order to solve each of their issues.

In 2014 I worked for the company SEGO as a translator of manuals and brochures for imported translation tools. I also traveled to Austria to perform consecutive interpretations with the company SOLA for training sessions on the use of tools for staff members. Since then, I have been working as a freelance English-Spanish teacher and translator.
